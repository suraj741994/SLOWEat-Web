How It Works

Experience the convenience of ordering from SlowEAT right at your fingertips! Our services are accessible through both our mobile app and website. Simply download our app for a seamless and user-friendly experience, or visit our website to place your order online. Whether you prefer the ease of a mobile app or the accessibility of our website, we've got you covered. Enjoy the simplicity of online ordering with SlowEAT, bringing the flavors of India to your doorstep with just a few taps or clicks.

  • 01 Download

    To get started, the initial step is to download our application, available on both the Play Store and the App Store. Simply visit the respective store for your device, and with a quick download, you'll have access to the fantastic features of our app. Explore the world of SlowEAT at your fingertips by initiating the download process on the Play Store for Android users or the App Store for iOS users.

  • 02 login & Select menu

    To place an order at our restaurant, kindly log in to your account and choose from our delectable menu items. Select your desired dishes to create a customized and satisfying dining experience tailored to your cravings. We appreciate your choice to dine with us, and the ordering process is made easy through your personalized login. Enjoy the convenience of selecting exactly what you desire to eat today at our restaurant.

  • 03 Wait for delivery

    Once your order is placed, anticipate a short wait for the delivery to your doorstep. Take this time to relax and get ready to savor a delightful meal from SlowEAT. We appreciate your patience, and soon enough, you'll be indulging in the culinary delights you've chosen. Enjoy the anticipation, and when your meal arrives, relish the flavors and satisfaction that come with each bite.
